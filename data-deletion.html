Data Deletion

Last updated: July 2026
Privacy Policy Imprint

Eight Gates is a local-first app: the data you create — diary entries, moods, voice notes, meals, workouts, hydration, habits, tasks, cycle data, weight and profile — is stored on your device, not on our servers. You are therefore in full control of it.

Delete your data yourself (immediately)

  1. Delete individual entries directly in the app at any time.
  2. Uninstall the app to permanently remove all data stored on your device.

Since there is no user account and your content is not stored on our servers, uninstalling the app removes your content completely from your side.

Data processed off the device

Only two kinds of data leave your device, and only for the feature you trigger:

  • Crash & diagnostic reports (via Sentry, EU/Germany region): technical information such as error type, stack trace, app version and device/OS. Configured without personal identifiers. Retained according to Sentry’s retention period and then deleted automatically.
  • AI feature content (via Google’s Gemini API): when you request an AI analysis, food photo recognition or AI search, the relevant content is sent to Google to fulfil that request and returned to your device.

Request deletion

To request deletion of any data associated with you — including any crash/diagnostic data — contact us:

Email: nodal.dev.official@gmail.com

Please use the subject line “Data deletion request (Eight Gates)”. We will process your request and delete the applicable data without undue delay, and in any case within 30 days.
